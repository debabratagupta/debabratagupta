I'm Debabrata Gupta.

Indian Classical Musician, Tabla.

Shri Debabrata Gupta

Shri Debabrata Gupta, is an eminent name in the field of Tabla, has born in a musical family. He started learning at his age of four under the great tabla player Sri Subhash Thakur and Sri Alok Saha of Delhi and Punjab gharana. Later he learnt from Sri Subrata Gupta of Farrukhabad Gharana. For his advance training he was under the able guidance of the great tabla maestro Late Pandit Shankar Ghose for fifteen years. He took special training of taal-laya from Pandit Bikram Ghosh as recommended by Guruji himself. He has been guided by senior gurubhai, Sri Amar Seth. After the death of Acharya Pandit Shankar Ghose, Debabrata started learning under Acharya Pandit Bapu Patwardhan, who is a senior disciple of Ustad Ahmed Jan Thirakwa. Apart from tabla, he learnt South Indian percussion from the famous Carnatic Artist Guru N. Shankar. To grow his musical sense and accompaniment on classical music, he also learnt playing sarod from Sri Amitava Mazumder.
